About Ninong Ry

Known for: Cooking videos with a humorous twist

Ryan Morales, better known as Ninong Ry, is a popular Filipino chef and vlogger. He gained widespread recognition for his entertaining cooking tutorials, often combining humor with culinary skills. His breakout video was on making Kare-kare during the COVID-19 pandemic.

He has collaborated with various celebrities and continues to inspire millions through his YouTube channel.

Timeline of Ninong Ry's Life
